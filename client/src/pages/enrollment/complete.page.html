Thank you for completing enrollment in the HeartSteps study.

Week 1

Wear your Fitbit for a week during waking hours. You may also use it while you sleep if you want to.

Week 2

Install and start using the HeartSteps app.

After you have worn your Fitbit for a week, you will receive a text message from the study with a link to the HeartSteps app. Please install the app on your phone. You will need your Entry Code and your birth year to login to the app. Refer to the instructions included in the mailing.
